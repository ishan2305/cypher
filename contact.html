FAQ

Common
questions

Do I need prior experience to enrol in a course? ▼
It depends on the course. Our beginner programs like CompTIA Security+ and CYPHER Red Team Associate require no prior experience. Intermediate and advanced courses like CEH, CPENT, and OSCP require foundational networking and OS knowledge. We assess every applicant and recommend the right entry point.
How long does it take to get certified? ▼
Timelines vary by course. Most of our programs run 4–12 weeks depending on study hours per week. Intensive full-time tracks can compress this significantly. We'll give you a realistic timeline based on your availability during your free consultation.
Do you offer instalment payment plans? ▼
Yes. Most courses offer 3 and 6-month instalment plans at no extra cost. Corporate packages can be invoiced quarterly. Contact us to discuss payment options for your specific situation.
What is the placement assistance process? ▼
All enrolled students get access to our careers platform, which includes resume reviews, mock interview sessions, LinkedIn optimization, and direct referrals to our network of 200+ hiring partners. Our placement team works with you until you land the right role — not just any role.
Can CYPHER deliver training at our offices? ▼
Absolutely. Our enterprise team delivers on-site training across UAE, India, UK, and the broader GCC and APAC regions. We bring the instructors, lab infrastructure, and course materials — you provide the space and the people.
How quickly can your IR team respond to an active breach? ▼
Our incident response SLA is 2 hours for remote deployment. For clients with an active retainer, we can begin triage within 30 minutes. On-site deployment timelines depend on location — our IR team is based in Dubai, Bangalore, and London for regional coverage.

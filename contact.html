FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete a course? ▼
Course duration varies by program. The Audit Professional course runs 8-10 weeks, while Offensive and Defensive Security courses typically run 10-12 weeks. All courses are available in self-paced or instructor-led formats.
Do you offer corporate training for teams? ▼
Yes! We offer customized corporate training programs for teams of any size. Our corporate packages include tailored curriculum, dedicated mentors, and flexible scheduling. Contact us for a custom quote.
What certifications will I receive? ▼
Upon successful completion of each course, you receive a CyLens Certificate of Completion. We also prepare you for globally recognized certifications such as CEH, OSCP, CompTIA Security+, and others depending on your chosen track.
Is there any prerequisite knowledge required? ▼
Our Audit Professional course is beginner-friendly with no prerequisites. The Offensive and Defensive Security courses recommend a basic understanding of networking and operating systems. We provide a free self-assessment to help you pick the right course.
How do I get started with your security services? ▼
Simply fill out the contact form above or email us directly at info@cylens.academy. Our team will schedule a free initial consultation to understand your requirements and propose a tailored solution.

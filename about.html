Our Mission

We think like
the enemy.
We train like the best.

CYPHER Academy was founded in 2015 by a team of former government red-teamers, CISO-level practitioners, and security researchers who were tired of watching organizations get breached because their defenders weren't trained on real threats.

Every course, lab, and curriculum update at CYPHER is driven by live threat intelligence. We rebuild our programs quarterly because the adversaries we're training against don't stand still — and neither do we.

Our Story

From a startup lab to
a global institution

What started as a 12-person cohort in Dubai has grown into a globally recognized academy with offices in Dubai, Bangalore, and London — and students in 45+ countries. Our accreditations from EC-Council, OffSec, CompTIA, ISC2, and ISACA make us one of the most credentialed cybersecurity training institutions in the MENA and South Asia region.

We've trained security teams for government agencies, Fortune 500 enterprises, and critical infrastructure operators. When organizations need people who can actually stop a breach — not just pass a compliance audit — they send their people to CYPHER.

Our Values

What we stand for

[◈]
Hands-On First

Every concept is taught through doing. We build cyber ranges, not lecture halls. Theory without practice is a liability in this field.

[◆]
Ruthlessly Current

Our curriculum is rebuilt quarterly using live CVE data, real breach reports, and threat intelligence from our network of active practitioners.

[◉]
Students First

From placement assistance to 1-on-1 mentorship, we measure success by where our graduates end up — not by enrollment numbers.

Ethical Practice

Community Driven
